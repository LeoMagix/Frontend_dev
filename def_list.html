The Power of The Mind

People who to me embody the power of harnessing your mind and mastering your thoughts.

Masters of the mind

Nikola Tesla
For me the greatest inventor that ever lived, and arguably the man with the most intelligent brain. Pioneered the AC current that powers all the worlds electrical appliances and many mind-bending innovations impossible to fathom. Dubbed, "Master of Lightening", he made a great impact on the field of Electrical Engineering.
Michael Jordan
A basketball player who willed himself into definiton of what the purest form of excellence in your work should look like. His dogged determination, plain refusal to settle for defeat, psychotic work ethic, coupled with a ruthless desire to win and utter disrespect for fear turned him into the most fearsome competitor.
Elon Musk
A modern day pioneer, genius and a example that every knowledge is acquirable if you really want to learn it. Highly resourceable, not scared of failure or scarred by mistakes, this attributes enable him to see beyond the stratosphere of impossible that limit many potential minds of today.
